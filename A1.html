Veteran Resources For CSULA Students

What you need to know

CSULA has a Veterans Resource Center (VRC) which serves as a community for military-connected students, providing academic and professional resources for student development and success. You may reach us at (323)343-5080 or at vets@calstatela.edu.

Financial Aid and Benefits

GI Bill: The Post-9/11 GI Bill provides financial support for tuition and fees, housing, and other educational expenses.

Yellow ribbon Program: This program allows participating schools to provide additional financial assistance to cover tuition and fees not fully covered by the GI Bill.
